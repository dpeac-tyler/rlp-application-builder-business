Document Upload

Define the documents included on application submissions.

  • First provide the applicant with instructions and information about the documents needed.
  • Then, add documents up to the maximum of 25. For each document, define details that will help constituents understand what is being requested and enable your organization to effectively manage the documents.
  • Later, in the Question Builder and Conditions sections, optionally map these documents to questions in your application. Documents that are part of conditions cannot be changed. To change them, first go to the Conditions tab and remove the condition(s). Then, return to this page and make any edits. Finally, return to the Conditions tab and reapply any rules. If you do not map a document to a question, then it will appear in a separate Document Upload section on the application.
